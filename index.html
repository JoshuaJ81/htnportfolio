Who I am

My name is Hoang Nguyen from Houston, Texas and I am a freelance photographer. I love the art of photography because I can capture special moments, and preserve them. Photography facilitates one's desire to reminisce on special places, experience, and people. I relish all opportunities to showcase the beauty of people, nature, and life.

Next

What I Do

I work for a world-class cancer hospital, MD Anderson Cancer Center. Outside of my work at MD Anderson, I have a passion for photography and videography. I specialize in taking pictures and making videos of life experiences to facilitate the needs of my clients, and to share said experiences via social media channels, such as YouTube. Also, I am a YouTuber and Vlogger who enjoys sharing my art with others, while inspiring other to learn the art of photography.
